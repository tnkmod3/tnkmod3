Short Introduction to Resumes/Cover Letters!



The Resume is the way you can get the interview. Your Cover Letter is an introduction to yourself to allow your employer to know more about you.

Many mistakes can be made on your resume, including spelling errors and formatting issues. If there is any sort of mistake, a lot of employers will throw away the resume on the spot.

Cover letters go over a lot of different information that you may not have a chance to explain in your resume. You get to introduce yourself as well as talk about what you can bring to the company. You can also showcase what you know about a certain company.
