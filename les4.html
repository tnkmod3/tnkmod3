6-Step Troubleshooting Theory



Using the A+ six-step troubleshooting theory you can organize your thoughts into a way that will help you solve the error. While there are only six steps there is a lot more in between. We plan to talk about all the different strategies to use while trying to troubleshoot a computer.

There are a lot of tech support jobs as well as general customer service jobs that will require you to troubleshoot computers/devices. This lesson will not teach how to troubleshoot problems, but how to troubleshoot effectively. This will save a lot of time scrambling around the problem instead of getting it organized and thinking about the problem before tackling it.
